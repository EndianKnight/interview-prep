# Vector Databases

Specialized databases for storing and querying high-dimensional vectors.

- **Purpose:** Similarity search (KNN/ANN).
- **Core Algorithms:** HNSW, IVF, LSH.
- **Tools:** Pinecone, Milvus, Weaviate, Qdrant, pgvector.
- **Use Cases:** RAG, semantic search, recommendation systems.
